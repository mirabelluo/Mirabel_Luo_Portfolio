Hopkins Student Wind Energy Team (HSWET)

As part of the national Collegiate Wind Competition, I led the implementation of our turbine's electronic safety system. The system used MOSFETs and sensor feedback to stop the turbine under unsafe conditions — all optimized for our custom-built design.

I’m proud to have contributed to our team placing 5th overall nationally, where I also presented in the technical design competition. This year, I’ll be stepping into the role of Electronic Controls Team Lead as we build a new prototype from the ground up!
